Jenny Saville

Jenny Saville is a member of the YBA (Young British Artists), born in Cambridge in 1970, renowned for her monumental paintings of women, she was enrolled at the Glasgow School of Art to finish her BA. During her residency in the University of Cincinatti, in the US she discovered the largest population of obesity. This became a subject of interest and remained a major visual theme in her works. Saville fell in love with the physicality of fat bodies; she found excessive flesh or obesity as a captivating subject. One of her main focus is on body image related issues, which is emphasized in her major paintings. People have established Saville as a feminist artist who conveys her message of concern on the social issues related to body image and appearance in her paintings.
