about membrane

Membrane explores the idea of an outer skin, not only as a protective mechanism or a barrier but as a beautified presence of layer. It explores the fragile and rigid nature of exteriority as a single layer of opacity, transparency and translucency, it embodies the shape of another being. Mainly referencing artist works that explore the physicality of the exterior and the interior, Membrane focuses on the aesthetic quality of outer skins, coverings, all exterior layers of being. Membrane serves as a guide to the contemporary world of exteriority providing narratives of artists and designers dealing with the subject, their explorations and iterations of the idea of membrane itself. This site is both interactive and informative, intended to guide the viewer to another realm of membrane through the visual architecture of layers structured on this site.
